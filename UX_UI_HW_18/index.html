Great design drives me.

I've created design, illustration and interactive digital experiences that are seen nationally and internationally. I've redesigned multiple US newspapers. I've spoken at SND (the Society of New Design) and won multiple awards from a bunch of AP's to a few SND's. But that's not my story.
My passion is driving the next iterations of great design and communication. I love the act of finding new solutions and innovative ways of engaging with readers and users, exciting them and leaving them hungry for knowledge and experiences they've yet to imagine.
